Overview

Executive Summary

Microforce, manufactured by Innidisa (Spain), is a professional-grade microcement system with strong credentials in Spain and growing European distribution. Microstone Ltd holds the exclusive UK distribution rights and is opening a dedicated showroom in Newcastle-under-Lyme, co-located with Designworks in a shared design hub.

The opportunity: Microforce is essentially invisible in the UK market. There is no English-language web presence and no UK trade awareness. This is a first-mover advantage — Microstone can build the entire UK brand identity before any competitor establishes themselves in this space. The product category is growing fast, the timing is right, and the foundation is already in place.

The Product

Microforce Rustik: two-component, ready-to-use decorative microcement. Waterproof, highly adhesive, full colour range. Full system includes Micrex Diamond (heavy traffic epoxy) and a dedicated pool coating line. Innidisa backs with a 3-year guarantee (5 years on pool systems).

Existing Assets

Active Facebook and Instagram accounts. A distinctive, premium logo. An exclusive UK distribution agreement with the manufacturer. A physical showroom opening in Newcastle-under-Lyme, co-located with Designworks. An established online partner in Home of Clay (homeofclay.com).

What’s Missing

A UK domain and website. A structured content and SEO strategy. Trade directory listings. A UK-facing brand narrative in English. Sample packs in the hands of designers and installers. All of this can be built at low cost.

Partnership

Home of Clay & Designworks: Partnership Strategy

Home of Clay (homeofclay.com) is an established online-only tile retailer with a strong e-commerce presence and years of domain authority. Though there is no public showroom, it shares the same building as Designworks and the incoming Microstone showroom in Newcastle-under-Lyme — making this a natural cluster of complementary businesses. Home of Clay’s website is a valuable SEO and digital asset for Microforce UK.

Domain Authority & Backlinks

A Microforce product page or blog post on homeofclay.com passes established SEO trust to microstoneuk.com. New domains need backlinks from trusted sites — this is natural, legitimate, and geographically relevant.

Geographic Relevance

Home of Clay, Designworks, and Microstone all share the same building in Newcastle-under-Lyme. This cluster of related businesses is highly credible to Google for local search — backlinks between them carry real weight.

Existing Customer Audience

Home of Clay customers buying large-format tiles and bathroom stone are exactly the right audience for microcement — a natural product pairing that sits within the same purchase journey.

Product Cross-Selling

Microcement used alongside tile for feature walls or wet room floors is a natural product pairing. Home of Clay’s online customers buying tile are exactly the right audience to be introduced to Microforce.

Physical Sample Display

Home of Clay can display Microforce colour samples, routing trade enquiries to Microstone. Passive lead generation at zero ongoing cost.

Email Newsletter

A brief Microforce mention in Home of Clay’s existing e-commerce newsletter reaches a warm, relevant online audience immediately — directing them to visit the new showroom in person.

Suggested Implementation (Non-Intrusive)

The goal is to add genuine value to both businesses without overstepping. These steps are supportive, not disruptive, and keep full ownership and direction with Microstone.

Real-World Brand Building

Microstone Showroom at Designworks & Trade Outreach

A physical showroom is one of the most powerful sales tools for a surface finish product. Microstone’s new space within the Designworks building in Newcastle-under-Lyme creates a genuine design destination — combining tile, stone, interior design, and microcement under one roof. The following are offered as supportive suggestions.

Colour & Texture Panel Wall

Mounted boards showing Rustik in every available colour, applied over different substrates. Visitors run their hand across the finish — this sells the product better than any brochure.

Substrate Comparison

Side-by-side samples showing microcement applied directly over tile, concrete, and plasterboard. Makes the “no demolition needed” story visual and tangible.

Pool System Corner

Even a small display of the aquatic coating opens a premium revenue line and differentiates Microstone from any other showroom in the region.

QR Codes on Every Sample

Links to the product page, application video, and TDS download. Bridges the physical visit with the digital brand — every sample becomes a lead capture tool.
